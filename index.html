FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to provide my own content? +

No — we create branded graphics and captions for you. However, if you have photos or videos of your business, we encourage you to share them as they perform better than generic graphics.

Which platforms do you manage? +

We manage Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok. The number of platforms depends on your package — Starter covers 1, Growth covers 2, and Premium covers 3.

How long is the contract? +

All packages are month-to-month. You can cancel with 14 days notice (30 days for Premium). We recommend a 3-month commitment for best results and offer a 10% discount for that.

When do I pay? +

Full payment is due before work begins each month. We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and other payment methods. We'll send you an invoice at the start of each billing cycle.

How do I approve content before it goes live? +

We send you a content batch for approval before scheduling. You have 48 hours to review and request changes. We include 2–3 rounds of revisions depending on your package.

Do you run paid ads? +

Paid ads are available as an add-on to any package. The ad budget is paid directly by you to Facebook/Instagram. We handle the campaign setup, targeting, and management.
